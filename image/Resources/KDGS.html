The Kidney Disease Genetic Scorecard provides a comprehensive prioritization of GWAS variants associated with kidney function (eGFRcrea, N=2.2 million individuals), including functional variants, target genes, and cell type-specific regulatory circuits.

Our manuscript describing Kidney Disease Genetic Scorecard was published as: Liu et al., Kidney multiome-based genetic scorecard reveals convergent coding and regulatory variants. Science (2025). PMID: 39913582.
