This Zoo Genome Browser provides an integrated genome browser of over 50 rodent species collected by the PPG Project (Comparative Genomics of Longevity, P01AG047200) led by Prof. Vera Gorbunova in the University of Rochester.

This PPG project aims to identify molecular mechanisms responsible for the longevity of long-lived rodent species by using comparative genomics approaches.

The Zoo Genome Browser is constructed based on the UCSC Genome Browser. This website is maintained by the Liu Lab in the University of Rochester.
